class MultiHeadAttention(nn.Module):
    def __init__(self, d_model, n_heads):
        super().__init__()
        self.attention = nn.Linear(d_model, d_model * 3)
        self.n_heads = n_heads

    def forward(self, x):
        # Compute Q, K, V projections
        qkv = self.attention(x)
        q, k, v = qkv.chunk(3, dim=-1)

        # Scaled dot-product attention
        scores = torch.einsum('bhqd,bhkd->bhqk', q, k)
        attn = F.softmax(scores / math.sqrt(d_k), dim=-1)
        return torch.einsum('bhqk,bhvd->bhqd', attn, v)

About Me

I'm a Computer Science student at the University of Maryland, College Park, pursuing my passion for understanding how machines can truly comprehend human language.

My research interests lie at the intersection of symbolic AI and neural networks—developing hybrid architectures that combine the interpretability of classical methods with the power of deep learning. I'm particularly focused on building language systems that go beyond surface-level pattern matching to achieve genuine semantic understanding.

Currently conducting research on post-quantum cryptography in the Gemstone Honors Program and have contributed to media bias detection at MBZUAI. My long-term goal is to pursue a PhD and build AI systems that can reliably detect misinformation and omission in text.
